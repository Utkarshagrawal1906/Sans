Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

Thank you for using the Loan and Interest Calculator app. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how our application handles information.

Summary: We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data on external servers. All calculation data remains strictly on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Our app is designed to function without requiring you to create an account or provide any personal information (such as your name, email, or phone number). We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

2. Local Storage of Data

The application allows you to save your calculation history for your own convenience. This data, which includes principal amounts, interest rates, and terms, is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.

  • History Management: You have full control over this data. You can delete individual records or clear your entire history at any time within the app settings.
  • Access: This information is only accessible through the app on your physical device.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking cookies. We do not sell or share any information with third parties.

4. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on the security of your mobile device itself. We recommend using standard device security features (like PINs or biometric locks) to protect your data.
